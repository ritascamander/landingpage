Production

These are raw images of what is going on behind the scenes.
The work is mostly fun with small breaks to catch up with sunlight and get some fresh air. All of our products are made with care and detail which makes all the pieces unique. We use the finest materials in the market and try to be as much sustainable as possible. Most of the times we use vintage materials or fabrics have no continuity, which makes all of the collections limited. This means that when we finish the stock those pieces will not be back to the collection, so we are continuously working on new designs.

Who are we? Who I am?

These are some great and old philosophical questions. Although, I can only answer to who are we  - LAURIE.

The brand started in March of 2020 during the beginning of the pandemic as a way of keeping the sanity while living without knowing what the future could be. I created the first bag and quickly got really good feedback from the people around me. I started investing on this idea and soon realized it was something I like to do, as it takes my mind into creative spaces and out of the problems of the physical world. 

The concept is pretty simple: these are unique pieces made for people who appreciate the magic of handmade work and also that understand that it is better to have less things in the closet, but things that will last for a long time. We try as much to be sustainable in our choices and are fully transparent in our decisions. All the bags are of limited edition.

The LAURIE team is mainly myself doing the little bags with my very own hands, with the help of my seamstress fairies.

Thank you for supporting us!

Rita Azevedo

LAURIE founder, designer and artisan

FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions / Perguntas Frequentes

Do you have a Shop? / Tem loja?

We do not have a physical shop and our focus is the online market. Although we also do appointments with clients in our Showroom at Retrosaria da Vila, in vila do Conde. / (PT) Não, não temos loja física e o nosso foco é a venda online. No entanto, temos um Showroom em Vila do Conde na Retrosaria da Vila, por marcação.

 What is the average product price? / Qual é o preço médio dos vossos produtos?

The price range is currently between 30€ to 200€, depending on the type of bag. We have simple bags ,for example, Totes and more handmade complex products. / (PT) O preço atual varia entre os 30€ e os 200€ dependendo da complexidade do produto. Temos sacas simples, por exemplo Tote Bags e outras carteiras com execução manual mais trabalhada.

Where are the products made? / Onde são feitos os produtos?

The products are handcrafted in Porto, Portugal in a smart studio by the brand designer Rita Azevedo with the help of seamstress professionals. / (PT) Os produtos são feitos à mão no distrito do Porto, Portugal, num pequeno estúdio, pela designer da marca - Rita Azevedo - com a ajuda de costureiras profissionais.

 Can I physically see the bags before ordering? / Posso ver as carteiras fisicamente antes de encomendar?

Yes, you can see the bags in person by scheduling a meeting in our Showroom at Retrosaria da Vila, in Vila do Conde. / (PT) Sim, pode ver as carteiras pessoalmente por marcação, no nosso Showroom, na Retrosaria da Vila, em Vila do Conde.

 Do you make personalized bags? / Fazem carteiras personalizadas? 

Yes, we do make personalized bags by an extra fee with the materials we have. For example you can order a personalized bag for an event, wedding, gala, etc. / (PT) Sim, fazemos carteiras personalizadas com os materiais que temos por um valor acrescido. Por exemplo, pode encomendar uma carteira personalizada para um evento, casamento, gala, etc.

 How can I order a bag? / Como posso encomendar uma carteira?

Currently you can order by Direct Message on Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger or with the Paypal Button - Pay Now. / Pode encomendar a sua carteira por mensagem no Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger ou através do Botão Paypal - Pay Now.
