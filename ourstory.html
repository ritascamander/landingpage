Our Story

The beginning

LAURIE - Handcrafted Bags, started in March of 2020 during the beginning of the pandemic as a way of keeping the sanity while living without knowing what the future could be. I created the first bag and quickly got really good feedback from the people around me. I started investing on this idea and soon realized it was something I like to do.

The brand comes as an outcome of two generations of haberdashery family business owners. These are unique pieces 100% handcrafted in Portugal and made for people who appreciate the sublime art of handmade work and also that understand that it is better to have less things in the closet, but things that will last for a long time. We aim to be more and more sustainable and our materials are all of vegan origins. All the bags are of limited edition which means you will be one of a few people having the same bag in the whole word, and still it is never the same bag because it is handmade.

Each individual is unique and deserves to celebrate this uniqueness.

Thank you for supporting us!

Rita Azevedo

LAURIE founder, designer and artisan

Production

All of our products are 100% handmade in Portugal with care and detail which makes all the pieces unique. We use the finest materials in the market and try to be as much sustainable as possible. We also give preference to vegan leathers and fabrics. Most of the times we use vintage materials or fabrics have no continuity, that is mostly why we make limited collections. This means that when we finish the stock those pieces will not be back to the collection, so we are continuously working on new designs. It also means that your bag will be made for you and only for you.

Up to 6h hours of labour work

Our prices are adjusted to the labour work that is done to conceive your LAURIE bag.
Each piece can have a slightly different shape as it is molded by our hands.

Limited Collections

We make only a few models of each design which makes the product more exclusive and special.

Sustainable Choices

Although we buy materials for our collections we try to be sustainable when making decisions. We aim to start using waste/recycled materials in some of the future collections.

Vegan Products

This is a statement: all of our products are vegan. No textile, fur or leather is made with/of animal products.
